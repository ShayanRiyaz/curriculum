# Writing the code

You are keeping track of all the possible sequences and applying the restrictions (no 2 consecutive 'S' nor 2 'F').

Don't forget to mod calculations by 10 ** 9 + 7

For a dynamic programming approach, use this table

```
     |  0  |  1  |  2  |  3  | ... | n
------------------------------
H    |  1  |  1  |  2  |  4  |
------------------------------
S    |  0  |  1  |  1  |  2  |
------------------------------
SS   |  0  |  0  |  1  |  1  |
------------------------------
sh   |  1  |  2  |  4  |  7  |
------------------------------
```

Create variables for all previous 'H', 'S', 'SS', and "sh" and try to find an iterative (or recursive) approach
